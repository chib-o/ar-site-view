Welcome to Risk Pools!

Risk pools in the app is where:

  • You can manage risk assessments to be used as templates for your inspections

In RiskMach's risk pools you can:

  • Download existing risk pools
  • Make changes to pools by adding new pool risks or editing existing pool risks offline
  • Link pool risks to specific checklist questions to help prompt users during an audit
  • Upload your pool changes so others can use your pool risk assessments
